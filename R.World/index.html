RISHAB WORLD

SINCE 1929

Our Group has an experience of international trade in textiles for over 40 years, be it exports or imports. Our strength lies in access to the best of textile and garment factories across the globe. Our presence in international market reflects in our products and our fashion forward approach.

We have a group of inhouse brands specialising in varieties of Menswear Suiting fabric. The Fabric range includes PV Suitings, Polywool Suitings and Cotton,PolyCotton Shirtings. All the fabrics are sourced Internationally and distributed over a network of 4000 premium retailers and family shops by our exclusive agents. Our house brands include an array of products catering to diferent occasion and needs of customers. In 2001 India was introduced to our Italian flagship brand Don and Julio which is a partywear-ocassion wear brand. Vercelli, Italian Channel, Arok are a few other brands from our basket.
